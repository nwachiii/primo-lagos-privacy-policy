PRIMO LAGOS PRIVACY POLICY

1. INTRODUCTION

We take your privacy seriously and are committed to safeguarding your personal information at Primo Lagos Ltd (which term includes all subsidiaries of Primo Lagos Ltd) (the “Company,” “we,” “us,” or “Primo Lagos”). We value you as our customer, respect your privacy, and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

This policy describes the sources of your personal information that we may collect from you or that you may provide to us, our purposes for collecting and processing your information, and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, retaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or your personal information, please contact us using the details provided in the section “How do you contact us?”

2. WHAT ARE THE SOURCES OF YOUR PERSONAL DATA?

The sources of your personal data (collectively referred to as “Platforms” in this policy) may include, without limitation, the following:

  • Primo Lagos websites: These are websites operated by us under our own domains, web addresses (URLs), and micro-sites that may be part of a third-party social media network such as Facebook, Twitter, or Instagram.
  • Emails, text messaging services, and other electronic messages: These involve electronic text-based interactions between you and Primo Lagos.
  • Primo Lagos mobile applications: These are smartphone applications created for you and operated by Primo Lagos or one of our partners.
  • Primo Lagos customer service: Any communication between you and our customer service center (e.g. phone, chatbot, email, WhatsApp, etc.).
  • Online/Offline registration forms: These include printed or digital registrations, surveys, or questionnaires Primo Lagos collects via registration forms, contests, events, and promotions when you participate in one of our events or visit our promotional stands in malls to inquire about our properties.
  • Advertising: You might interact with one of our or our partners’ advertisements on our websites and apps, and we might receive and collect this information.
  • Career Portal: This includes recruitment portals across Primo Lagos.
  • Data we create: Primo Lagos may also create data about you, for instance when you use or avail yourself of our services.
  • Data from other sources: These include social media networks, market research agencies, Primo Lagos third-party sales agents, registered brokers, Primo Lagos promotional partners, public sources, and data received when we acquire other companies.
  • Primo Lagos Loyalty Program: Once you register for our loyalty program, we collect and process your personal information and transaction history.
  • Primo Lagos Public Wi-Fi: Any usage of the Primo Lagos public Wi-Fi network at our registered offices.

This policy does not apply to information collected:

  • through any other means, including any other website operated by a third party; or
  • by any third party, including through any application or content (including advertising) that may link to the Website.
